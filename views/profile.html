  • Gourmet Tuna Sandwich

    Tuna salad sandwiches are pretty basic and often disappointing… but this one is different. Not only does it consist of ingredients from a tuna salad but from a Waldorf salad as well. A quick glance at the ingredient list will tell the story if this sandwich is right for you. The crunch from the apple and celery add a nice touch to this sandwich, and the Swiss cheese tops it off nicely. There is a bit of prep work required but it is worth it. This recipe is perfect for summer and it can be prepared in advance for picnics. Enjoy.

  • Almond Cookies

    This alternative recipe for almond cookies is courtesy of Martha Lindsay, MS, CNE, our team member who specializes in nutrition education and nutrition response testing. The recipe, ideal for anyone experiencing symptoms from a concussion, stroke, or brain injury of any kind, as well as ADD/ADHD, Parkinson’s or MS, let’s you treat yourself to something sweet without having to pay for it after the fact. All the ingredients work for your brain, not against it. Proceed without fear of putting your brain and body in a tizzy, and give these goodies a try!
